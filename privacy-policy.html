Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 11 September 2025

GreenNest ("we," "us," or "our") operates the GreenNest website (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with transparency and care. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, in compliance with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us and information that is automatically collected when you use our Service.

Information You Provide to Us

  • Contact Information: When you use our contact form, we collect your name and email address to respond to your inquiries.
  • AI Planner Content: When using our AI-powered features, we collect the information you submit, which may include:
    • Text descriptions of your garden, space, or agricultural questions.
    • Images of your space that you voluntarily upload.
    • Voice data if you use the microphone feature, which is transcribed into text.
    • Your selected province for localized advice from the AI Plant Advisor.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Log and Usage Data: Our servers may automatically collect standard data provided by your web browser, such as your device's IP address, browser type and version, the pages you visit, the time and date of your visit, and the time spent on those pages.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, grounded in our legitimate interest to operate and improve our Service:

  • To Provide and Maintain our Service: To operate our website, respond to your contact requests, and provide the core functionality of our AI tools.
  • To Personalize Your Experience: The content you provide to our AI tools is used to generate personalized garden plans and advice tailored to your specific context.
  • To Improve Our Service: We analyze usage data to understand how our visitors use the website, which helps us improve its functionality, content, and user experience.
  • For Communication: We use your contact information to communicate with you in response to your inquiries and to provide important updates about our services.

3. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with third parties only in the specific circumstances described below:

  • Third-Party AI Service Providers: To power the AI Garden Planner and AI Plant Advisor, we send the content you provide (text, images, voice transcriptions) to a third-party generative AI service (Google Gemini API). This data is processed by them to generate the response you receive. We recommend you review Google's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle data.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency) in South Africa.
  • Protection of Rights: We may disclose information where we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding potential violations of our policies, suspected fraud, or situations involving potential threats to the safety of any person.

4. Data Retention and Security

We retain the personal information we collect for as long as necessary to provide the services you have requested, and thereafter for a variety of legitimate legal or business purposes. We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

5. Your Data Protection Rights under POPIA

As a resident of South Africa, you have the following rights under POPIA:

  • The right to be notified that your personal information is being collected.
  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you.
  • The right to request correction of any inaccurate information or deletion of your personal information.
  • The right to object to the processing of your personal information.
  • The right to lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa if you believe we are processing your data unlawfully.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • By Email: contact@greennest.co.za
